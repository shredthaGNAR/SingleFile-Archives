Frequently Asked Questions

What is WebUI?

WebUI is a user-friendly chat interface that allows you to interact with AI models using your HelixMind API key.

Is there an additional cost?

No, WebUI access is included with your HelixMind subscription at no additional cost.

I forgot my password. What should I do?

Simply click the "Create WebUI Account" button again to regenerate your account credentials.

Does this count against my API usage?

Yes, any interactions through WebUI will count toward your daily API request limit.

Why it's loading forever?

After logging into WebUI, please refresh that page by clicking the refresh icon in your browser to make it work properly.
