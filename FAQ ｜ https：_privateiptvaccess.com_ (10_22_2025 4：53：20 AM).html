A Guide to Using M3U4U.com EPG

Introduction:

M3U4U.com is a web-based service that allows users to create and manage electronic program guides (EPG) for IPTV services. This guide is designed for beginners, making it easy to understand and use M3U4U.com for managing EPG data.

Step 1: Register and Sign In

  1. Open your web browser and go to https://m3u4u.com.
  2. Click on the "Sign Up" button to create an account if you don't already have one.
  3. Follow the registration process, including providing your email, creating a password, and agreeing to the terms of service.
  4. Once registered, sign in to your M3U4U.com account.

Step 2: Add Your M3U Playlist

  1. After signing in, you will be taken to your dashboard.
  2. Click on "Playlists" in the top menu.
  3. Select "Add Playlist."
  4. Enter a name for your playlist and paste the M3U URL provided by your IPTV service provider.
  5. Click "Save Playlist."

Step 3: Add EPG Data

  1. Go back to your dashboard by clicking on the "Dashboard" link in the top menu.
  2. You will see your playlist listed. Click on the "EPG" icon.
  3. Click "Add EPG" and select a source for your EPG data.
  4. Enter the URL (EPG URL from your https://m3u4u.com account) or upload a local XML EPG file.
  5. Click "Add EPG Source."

Step 4: Match Channels

  1. Once your EPG source is added, you'll be taken to the "Match Channels" page.
  2. Here, M3U4U.com will attempt to match your channels to the EPG data. Review the matches and make any necessary adjustments by selecting the correct EPG channel for each M3U channel.
  3. Click "Save."

Step 5: Generate Merged Playlist

  1. Return to your dashboard.
  2. Click on the "Playlists" tab.
  3. Click "Actions" for your playlist and choose "Generate Merged Playlist."
  4. Review the settings and click "Generate Merged Playlist" to create a new M3U URL with EPG data.

Step 6: Update Your IPTV Player

  1. Open your preferred IPTV player on your device (e.g., VLC, TiviMate, IPTV Smarters).
  2. In the settings or playlist section, replace the old M3U URL with the new merged M3U URL generated by M3U4U.com.
  3. Save the changes and reload the playlist.

Step 7: Enjoy Your IPTV Service with EPG

  1. You've successfully added EPG data to your IPTV service.
  2. Browse channels with program information, show schedules, and more.

Tips:

  • Regularly update your EPG data for accurate programming information.
  • M3U4U.com also provides a handy feature to schedule EPG updates automatically.

Conclusion:

With M3U4U.com, managing electronic program guides for your IPTV service is a straightforward process. By following this guide, even beginners can easily enhance their viewing experience with EPG data. Enjoy your favorite TV shows and channels with the added benefit of program information.
