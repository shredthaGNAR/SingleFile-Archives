When making calls, voicemail is usually found or heard. If you get short voicemail greetings, it means the person you’re calling is unable to pick up the phone. You can, however, hit the requested button, and then leave a message for the person you are contacting. According to Duthler’s study, one of the goals of creating voicemail greeting is to reduce the distinction between manipulating electronic mail and voice communications. You may activate or create a voicemail greeting by using the program you have or by using a voicemail greeting generator. By using a generator, you can create a greeting whatever you like and dubbed by other person. In addition, these greetings are usually used for companies, so the language is more formal. However, you can also make your phone greetings in your own style informally. Actually the greeting made for the voicemail is not always short, but it can be a bit long. Here are some tips on how to make good voicemail greetings. Introduce self – It doesn’t have to be too long, at least say your name, so the callers know they are calling the right person. Not stiff – When doing voicemail greetings, avoid using a monotonous tone like a robot. Just use your voice as creatively as possible. Make a script – You can first write down the greetings you want to avoid forgetting or speaking incorrectly when recording. Creative voicemail greetings will be more interesting to listen to. Updates – Do regular updates on your greetings, so your callers will not be bored and listen to your messages thoroughly. Give reassurance – Providing certainty to the caller is also one of the important things that need to be given. By providing certainty such as when you will call back, the callers can patiently wait for your answer or call. Slowly – When recording for voicemail, it’s best to do it slowly, so the caller can catch the message you convey. Duration – Make sure you keep the message short and clear. If you really want it to be a bit longer, that’s OK. But, as much as possible, don’t exceed 25 seconds, so the caller doesn’t have to listen to your message over and over again. The most common ones are funny voicemail greetings. Lots of greetings are made using funny words or funny pronunciation tones. This cute greeting style will make many people listen to your message until the end, and it also makes the listener more entertained.
