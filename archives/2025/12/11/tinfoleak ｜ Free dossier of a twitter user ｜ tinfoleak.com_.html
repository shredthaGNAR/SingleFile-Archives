About

Tinfoleak.com is a website where you can get detailed info about a Twitter user.

Tinfoleak uses geo information that appears in tweets and uploaded images to locate the place where the user was when he tweeted something

Tinfokeak.com provides a web interface for the OSINT tool "Tinfoleak", authored by Vicente Aguilera Diaz (@VAguileraDiaz)
