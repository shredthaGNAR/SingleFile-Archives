Shortcuts Library

Shortcuts, and its predecessor, Workflow, have been a game changer to anyone who has wanted to take their use of iOS and iPadOS to a new level. We’ve been working with these apps from their first release, and over the years we’ve learnt how to do quite a few things with them, sharing literally hundreds of workflows and shortcuts with users around the world.

This library of shortcuts is where we’ll be collating the shortcuts we’ve created, and sharing them with the public. The shortcuts are provided as is, but we hope you’ll find some actively productive shortcuts in here as well as lots of great examples that will help you with developing your own shortcuts.
