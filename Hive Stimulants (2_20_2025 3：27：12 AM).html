       Step by Step Recrystallization of Meth     

Step by step dual solvent recrystalization of Methamphetamine:

Although in the search engine there are many methods available, and many excellent threads regardig recrystalization of your percipitated methamphetamine hcl. There is not a step by step method with clear directions. I took much experiemnting, and searching to arrive at the method below. Worlock's Post: Dual Solvent Recrystalization: A leap ahead, and Rhodium's Recrystaliztion FAQ provide alot of informtion regarding this proceedure; although the Recryst FAQ are very informing they are not specific for methamphetamine, and do not give specific instructions. The Worlock Method uses methanol and after experimentation it has been determined that although the method is very accurate, and works well there are others that work even more effeciently. Below is a step by step mehod that will yeild excellent result. This method is only regarding recrystaliztion of a Meth Hcl. So you will have had to have done the following before applying it. Done a proper A/B of product, and then percipitated the products as an hcl salt (gassed the product.) From that point the product should have been vacuum filtered (buchner funnel), and then air dried. The air dried product is then used in this proceedure.

Step by step Methamphetamine Recrystalization:

Materials:
Isopropyl Alcohol (IPA)
Acetone
Epsom Salts MgS04
Methamphetamine HCl
Filter Discs or Coffee filters
Sharpie Pens
Masking Tape (For labeling)

Equipment:
Erlenmyer Flask
Beaker or Mason Jar
Hot plate Heat Source
3 Glass Storage Bottles
Freezer Funnel (buchner preffered)
Mist Bottle

First proceedure: Preparing the bottles and MgSO4:

1. Bake 100g MgSO4 in oven on low heat until they turn very white.
2. Place all 3 glass storage bottles into the oven on 350F for 30 minues:
3. Remove MgS04 to one of the bottles , cover and label Dry Epsom.
4. Cover the two remaining bottles, and label on DRY Acteone, and the other Dry IPA

Second Proceedure: Drying the solvents:

DANGER: This involves boiling solvents, this produces very flamable conditions in the lab while this is taking place, be sure to use a source of ventiliation with a spark proofed (explosion proof)fan, or a fume hood. Solvent vapors may create trails of vapor that may be ingnited and followed back to the container, use caution.

1. Into your erlenmyer flask, pour 1L of Acetone to be dried (may be scale up or down with same ratios)
2. Add 10g DRY Epsom per liter acetone.
3. Place flask onto hot plate, and heat to boiling, hold at boil for 10 minutes, then cover and cool to room temperature.
4. Pour dry acetone into the prepared container labeled dry acetone.
5. Repeat proceedure with IPA. (also may be scaled up or down with same ratios)
6. Store Dry IPA into prepared container

Third Proceedure recrystalization:
1. Place Dry acetone storage bottle into freezer or ice bath for 1 hour.
2. Pour Dry IPA ( 10ml IPA per 10g meth) into erlenmyer flask
3. Heat IPA to 70-100C, and maintain temp
4. Place Meth hcl into a beaker
5. Add Very Slowly Hot IPA to meth beaker, add a SMALL amount and stir .
6. Add more Hot IPA, to just BEARLY dissolove. Dissolve only to a slurry, not a cler liquid.
7. Stir paste like solution
8. Add a few more drops of hot IPA
9. Stir
10. Add 10ml cold dry acetone per 10g of meth with a minimum addition of 100ml acetone. So add 100ml unless you are recrystallizing more then 100g meth.
11. Stir slightly
12. Cover Beaker with watch glass, or mason jar with cover.
13. Store or wash containers of dry solvents MgSO4, ect.

Fourth Proceedure Finalization:
1. Place Beaker into freezer for 6-8 hours
2. Remove beaker from freezer
3. Allow Beaker to raise to room temerature while still covered.
4. Set up for filteration either vacum of gravity.
5. Put Filters into funnel
6. Open Beaker and pour the crystals and solution onto the filter.
7. Filter off liquid and move crystals to a pyrex dish.
8. Save liquid into a glass bottle labeled Mother Liqour. 9. Air dry crystals with a fan blowing over (not on) them.

Last Step:
1. Into a mist bottle pour a portion of dry acetone
2. Mist crystals with acetone
3. Dry with fan
4. Store in the uissual way

Pitfalls and variations:
There are three problems that you may encounter with this proceedure.
1. Not Dry enough solvents
2. Too Much IPA being used
3. Not enough time to recryst

To solve these problems is simple.
1. Properly dry solvents as outlined
2. Only add enough IPA to form a paste type mixture not dissolve to a clear liquid.
3. Don't be a tweaker wait 8 hours. . . .

Variations:

Variation 1: Room Temperature
1. Do not place in freezer, allow to recryst. at room temperature for 48 hours.
Differnece: Bigger purer crystals
Theroy, the longer the mother liqour solution is allowed to flow around and through the forming crystals the more clean they will become.  The IPA loses it's grasp more slowly on the meth and the acteone washes it as it crystalizes at room temperature.

Variation 2: Methanol or Ethanol as solvent

1. These are much harder to dry then IPA, and also do not dissolve the meth as well as the IPA, however for what ever reason methanol especialy has a tendency to make much sharper longer crystals, where IPA make thicker less jagged crystals.

I hope that many will find this useful it is the exact method SWIM uses, in fact this is the checklist list SWIM uses modified into a deritional list.

Monograph for 100g:

30g of MgSO4 was placved into a 200C oven, and dried to whiteness. This Dried MgSo4 was added to a prepared oven dried bottle.  100ml of acetone was placed into a 500ml erlenmyer flask, and boiled for 10 minted with 10g of MgSO4.  The Resulting dried Acetone was stored in a prepared oven dried container.  100ml IPA was placed into an erlenmyer flask and boiled for 10 minutes with 10g Dired MgSO4 and then stored into a prepared oven dried container.  100ml of dry acetone was chiled to a temp of 0C.  100ml of dry IPA was heated to 70C using a hot plate.  100g Meth was added to a beaker.  Hot IPA was added with slow addtion and intermitten stirring to form a slurry of IPA/Meth.  Once the slurry was formed, 5ml more dry IPA was added and the mixture stirred.  100ml chilled acetone was then added to the slurry/solution and stirred lightly.  The Beaker was then covered with a watch glass.  The beaker was then placed into a freezer, and lowered to 0C for 8 hours.  At the 8 hour point the beaker was removed and raised to room temperature.  The crystals were recovered from the solution via buchner filteration.  The filterate solution was saved, and stored.  The crystal portion was then fan dried, and misted with dry acetone.  The resulting product was extremly pure highly crystalized methamphetamine.
