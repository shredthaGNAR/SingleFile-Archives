            NARKsafe™ DrugDetect™-F1 is For Testing Powders Only!
            Not For Use on Psychedelics.

            The results of this device are merely presumptive.
            All test results must be confirmed by an approved analytical laboratory! 
            Presumptive identification is generally recognized within our legal system as a component of probable cause. There is no drug identification system presently in use which completely eliminates the occurrence of false positives and false negatives.
