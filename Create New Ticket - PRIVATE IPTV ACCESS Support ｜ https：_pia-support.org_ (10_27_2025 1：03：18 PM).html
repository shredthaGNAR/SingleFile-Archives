You can watch PRIVATE IPTV ACCESS in two simple ways:

  • M3U Playlist (Use in your favorite IPTV app or player)

Option 1: Use the Web Player (Recommended)

The easiest way to watch! Works on Smart TVs, PCs, phones, and tablets no extra apps needed.

  1. Open your browser (Chrome, Edge, Safari, or Smart TVs browser).
  2. Go to: https://privateiptvaccess.com/auth/login
  3. Log in to your account.
  4. Click on Customize Playlist and choose your favorite categories.
  5. Click Open Web Player to start watching instantly.

Works on: Samsung/LG Smart TVs, Android phones, PC/Laptops, or any device with a browser.

Option 2: Use an M3U Playlist in Your Own Player

If you prefer using your own IPTV app, you can load your PRIVATE IPTV ACCESS playlist easily.

  1. Go to: https://privateiptvaccess.com/auth/login
  2. Log in to your account.
  3. Click Customize Playlist and select your categories.
  4. Download your M3U Playlist OR copy your DIRECT M3U URL.

Supported Players:

  • PC/Laptop VLC Media Player (free)
  • Android/iOS IPTV Smarters, TiviMate, GSE IPTV, MX Player
  • Smart TVs / Android Boxes TiviMate, Smart IPTV, IPTV Smarters

How to use:
Download Method: Import the downloaded M3U file into your player.
Direct Link Method: Copy & paste the DIRECT M3U URL into your players Add Playlist/URL option.
