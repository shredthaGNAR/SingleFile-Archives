    This post serves to document and share my experience with attempting to remove N-IBA from meth. I had a very detailed report that was nearing completion but my phone doed and I lost all of what I had written. So this first post I going to briefly describe my observations and dollowed by a detailed report when my frustration allows it. So I apologize form the lack of details in this post, it's just the way the cookie crumbles..

    Also I have not confirmed the contents of the bag so this post can not be used as proof of or fact of any sort. I really don't have concrete facts, just a theory and anecdotal supporting evidence.

    My goal was to gain a better understanding of the contents of the bag and prices. All came from the same source. I have been feeling the the supposed I'll effects of smoking N-IBA and I feel that they are very toxic and the negative sides just seem to get more profound with each subsequent dose. I decided learning to purify the meth would be wise and this Batch was expendable. I was going to toss it but I figure this is a great way to learn and not feel bad about loss.

    I started by crushing the contents into a fine powder and placed it in oven at 240F for about 20-30 min, in hopes of subliminating and MSM that could be mixed in as well.

    I them removed it from the oven and allowed it to cola bit before proceeding with the hot IPA and acetone Rex. I also used the freezer for rapid cooling and let sit undisturbed for roughly 2 hrs. Apon returning to be solution, I was pleasantly surprised with a significant crystal population. I then filtered the solution and set the crystals out to dry. Once dried I hit them with a quick acetone wash for good measure and allowed to dry again. Returning to the dried crystals I noticed 2 distinctly different formations. One type was flat and varied from roundish to rectangular with soft edges. The other was
    Long sharp crystals that were more 3D and rigid, and seemed to grow out as well as up from the base.

    At this point I had no idea of what was what so I tediously separated them accordongly in preparation of consumption. Starting with a taste test. The rounder was salty and the spikes were quite bitter. I chose to vape the spikes first and was greeted that that familiar euphoric rush one would expect from quality gear. I felt my confidence growing as well as my need to tell unmecessary stories and ramble.

    The spikes certainly packs a punch, the potentcy shot up dramatically while required dose was reduced significantly as my dose was roughly 1/4 of what I would dose for the unwashed. The come up was so intense I had to take a min to gather myself. I took a second equal dose and observed. I felt stimulated bit chill. After roughly 30 min I decided to see how I would react to some uncut that I set aside. Almost immediately I felt like I had sobered up and the negative sides of N-IBA came back in full force. After 30 min I realized the sides aren't fading and followed up with 2 additional doses of the washed product, following the same dose protocol. I felt like the meth was rapidly Purging the N-IBA from my body along with the nasty sides, replacing it with the awesome feels assosiated with quality gear. The rush lasted a few hours and the high lasted into the next day. Sleep was not an option for the following 24hours and I felt motivated the whole 24.

    To me this was a good indicator of success regarding removing NIBA from meth. I have seen a lot of contradicting posts regarding dual solvent Rex and it's effectiveness. There seemed to be 2 sides regarding the matter, those who claim it's not possibly and those that claim it is possible and they always end their post with "just filter the crystals from the solution and you are good to go". That is clearly not the case which leads me to believe there is a lot of regurgitating info with not relavant knowledge. I don't think most of the people posting like that have even attempted it and that's for both sides. I've only come across the threads claiming it is possible and provoided step by step info.

    Any way I felt like I should attempt it and share my results. I hope this report can be useful and not further the confusion regarding NIBA. Il post up a more detaild report in bit . In the mean time feel free add your 2c or criticism, constructive or otherwise and even to tell me I'm wrong if I am which I very well may be, I still have not identified the contents of the bag so take this info with a grain of salt .I'll post some pics too. This is my first post so bear with me..

    I have been encountering what I have gathered to be isopropylbenzlamine for the past few months, all from the same source. It had the same characteristics of what user reports describe to be N-IBA, visually, structurally as well as the effects of consumption. I am familiar with meth and this was indeed not meth. I also come to a conclusion on every prior*(auto correct change it to prices) batch, including what was used for the expirament.

    The contents were a mixed bag hat varied each time. I would describe the suspect substance as

    Visually- wet feeling and looking, large formations, smooth edges, and distinct bubbles trapped within and sometimes a crystal was inside the suspect crystal.

    Structurally- it was brittle and broke under a small bit of pressure, about as much as one would use to pick up salt with your finger. Small bubble, bubble wrap too more pressure to pop than was required to break the crystal. If you looked at it wrong it would break! When it broke, it would fracture into multiple pieces and it was easy to powder.

    When melted it was a highly viscous yellow liquid and would seem to evaporate after a couple heating cycles. It burned easy and it would take around 30 seconds to resolifify.

    Effects of consumption- overwhelming fatigue, consistent hunger, moderate headache above the eyes, joint and muscle aches, kidney area ached, bluing of the body, confusion. It did not produce any euphoria, motivation, creativity, confidence. And it seemed to block or significantly reduce the effects of meth, like wise, meth would seem to counteract the effects of isopropylbenzlamine if taken individually.

    The acetone was not dry or anhydrous as like wise the 91% IPA was straight out of an unopened bottle. It's not ideal to use wet solvents, I just wanted to see what would happen. Although I had some degree of success, I would still recommend drying the solvents.

    As stated before, I am not able to comfirm the presence of N-IBA, it's just my somewhat educated guess. After removing the impurities, all the negative side effects were removed as well. The process is easy and could possibly reduce the damage from consuming impurities/cuts.

    When I say phenylethylamine(s) I'm referring to the family of drugs classified under that name. The psychoactivity of phenylethylamines is deactivated or activated by changes in chirality and stereocenter of the molecule. We see this in examples such as the levoamphetamines and beta-ketones of which the vast majority have little to no stimulant or euphoric effects. Ingesting these things has one thing in common, reports of headaches, lethargy and confusion.


    Would have been great the run a marquis reagent test on the two separate crystal form post workup to get better identification. If there was no activity on the reagent test the crystals could have been further tested for the presence of isopropylbenzylamine by being hydrogenated over Palladium on carbon. If it were isopropylbenzylamine the reaction products would be a mixture of isopropylamine and toluene where as meth would not react. Would be great to have more solid ID information.
